Music studio gets a rebrand (and a website of course)

This Leeds based music studio, situated by water, requested an identity based around a warm, homely, log cabin. We designed a logo based on a sketch by the client and built the brand around the log cabin, using wood effects and autumnal colours to represent the cosy feel that was sought after.

The website contains a booking service, that gives bands the opportunity to book through he website and contains a content management system allowing the client to update new features easily..

.

The client also requested flyers, business cards and even coffee mugs as part of the branding package. We are currently working on guitar plectrums and bottle openers which will carry the same branding and direct clients towards the online booking service.
